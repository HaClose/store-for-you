Store for you

A new subscription service where you can try your favorite Japanese items.
You can bring samples of items to your home, such as those found in Japanese drugstores.

Our Concept

What are the benefits of shopping at a store? Knowing that it is genuine, knowing that it is the right product for you, and knowing what color it is. There are many benefits. However, when you try to buy a Japanese product, you cannot try it. We will deliver the drugstore shelves to your home. We will give you a tester when you choose the product you like. Just like a store, you can try it and then buy it. We will create a world where you can get your favorite products with greater confidence across countries.

Features

  • You can select up to 10 products you want per month
  • You can receive up to three items of one kind at a time.
  • Shipping is three times, 10th, 20th and the end of the month every month.

How to use

  1. Join for our service.
  2. Send message by email or Facebook with the product name of the product we want
  3. Try when the tester arrives.
  4. If you have a product you want, please go to the product page via email or Facebook message
