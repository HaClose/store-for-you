Store for you

The subscription service where you can try your favorite Japanese items.
You can get samples of Japanese items to your home, such as those found in Japanese drugstores.

Our Concept

What are the benefits of shopping at a store, not on internet?
Knowing that it is genuine, knowing that it is the right product for you, and knowing what color it is.
There are many benefits.
However, when you try to purchase Japanese items on internet, you cannot try it.
We will deliver many samples of Jpanese items, like shelves of a drugstore in Japan.
You can purchase them after trying.
We will create a world where you can get your favorite products with greater confidence across countries.

Features

  • You can get 10 samples of items you want per month.
  • Please select up to 10 samples. If it is less than 10, we will choose some recommended samples so that we will ship 10.
  • You can ask for three per kind.
  • The next month, you can choose the same kind of item.
  • We will ship your parcel on the 10th, 20th or the last day of every month.
  • The shipping date depends on the date you tell us what you want.

How to use

  1. 1. Please send the number of the sample you want through Facebook Messenger.
    Press the get started button or the lower right button to launch Facebook Messenger.
  2. 2. On Facebook Messenger, we confirm the number and name of the product you want and send a page for payment.
    When payment is completed, registration is complete. You look forward to receiving the parcel.
  3. 3. Let's use the sample after the package arrives. Then evaluate the product through the questionnaire we send.
    If you have the product you want, you can purchase it on our website, Beauty Box Japan.
  4. 4. You consider the samples you want to try next month and let us know the item number.

FAQ

Q: How much is shipping fee?

Q: How long will it take for the product to arrive?

Q: There aren't the items I want...
A: Make your request on the contact form or Facebook Messenger. We strive to get them.
Q: I could not receive the products. What should I do?
A: The company we are partnering with is alliance with post office of the country you live.  After we ship the products, we will give you the tracking number. Please contact the post office which ship them.
Q: How long is the items expired date?
A: It depends on the items, but Japanese beauty goods are mostly over three years.
Q: How many times can a sample be used?
